Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026  ·  Gibraltar Bat  ·  Texas, USA

Gibraltar Bat is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using our website or placing an order, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect the following information that you provide directly:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Order notes or special requests

We do not collect or store your payment card information. All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe, Inc. We only receive confirmation that a payment was completed. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Process and fulfill your order
  • Contact you about your order status
  • Handle returns, exchanges, or warranty claims
  • Respond to questions or inquiries you send us

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service in connection with orders:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe may collect and process payment data independently under their own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain order information for as long as necessary to fulfill orders, handle warranty claims within the applicable window, and comply with any legal or tax obligations. If you would like your information removed from our records, contact us at the email below and we will accommodate your request where legally permitted.

Cookies

Our website uses browser local storage to save your shopping cart between visits. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. No personal information is stored in your browser — only your cart contents.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, request corrections, or request deletion. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@gibraltarbat.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@gibraltarbat.com
